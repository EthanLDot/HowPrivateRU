How Private Are You?

Welcome to How Private Are You?, a quick quiz to see if the tech you are buying respects your privacy or not.

According to MarketWatch, 2.5 quintillion bytes of data are created daily, amounting to at least $44 billion worth of information sold to other companies. If you are event slightly irked by the idea of your activity being sold off to the highest bidder, this 5-minute quiz is worth taking.

For all questions, indicate which devices you currently own or plan to have within the next six months. The data is analyzed to create a summary of results, but will not be stored.
